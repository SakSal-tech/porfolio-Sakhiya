How my lessons work

I assess current level, identify gaps, and create a personalised learning plan with clear targets. Lessons are adapted to learning style (visual, auditory, kinaesthetic), and complex concepts are broken down step by step.

For GCSE and A-Level students, teaching is aligned to the exam board specification with regular exam paper practice. Coding practice is scaffolded so students learn to write, test, debug, and improve their code.
